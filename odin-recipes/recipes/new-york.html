New York Roll

ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 3 Tbsp sugar
  • 3 Tbsp cornstarch
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 Tbsp butter
  • 1 egg
  • 1 Tbsp brown sugar
  • 4 Tbsp melted chocolate
  • 1 Tbsp hazelnuts

step by step recipe

  1. Prepare the pastry cream:Mix the egg with half the sugar and the cornstarch.
  2. Boil the milk, the other half of the sugar and the vanilla in a saucepan.
  3. Pour half the milk into the egg + sugar + cornstarch mixture, mix well then pour everything into the saucepan and put back on the heat.
  4. Remove from the heat then add the butter cut into small pieces. Mix well and film "on contact" to prevent a crust from forming. Leave to cool completely.
  5. Brush with beaten egg and sprinkle brown sugar on top. Place the greased cookie cutters around the spirals. Cover with a gratin dish.
  6. Take out of the oven and let cool down. Prick a roll twice on the slice with a chopstick and garnish with the pastry cream.
  7. Melt chocolate and decorate the edge of the roll, covering the two holes. Sprinkle with crushed hazelnuts. Repeat the operation with the second roll.
