Pumpkin Roll

ingredients

  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons pumpkin pie condiment
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2/3 cup pumpkin puree

step by step recipe

  1. Mix the flour, baking powder, baking soda, pumpkin pie spices, and salt in a large mixing bowl.
  2. Beat eggs and granulated sugar for 1 minute until thick. Mix the flour mixture with a rubber spoon and stir together until completely homogeneous.
  3. Distribute the mixture evenly in a prepared pan. Bake for 13 to 15 minutes, or until the top of the cake comes back when touched. Transfer cake roll to a wire rack and let it cool to room temperature.
  4. While the cake is cooling, make the cream cheese filling.
  5. Once the cake has reached room temperature, transfer the cake roll to a flat surface, making sure it is flat again. Roll the pumpkin tightly in plastic wrap and place in the refrigerator for at least an hour.
  6. Unwrap the pumpkin wrap, and transfer to a serving plate. Sprinkle the pumpkin roll gently on all sides with powdered sugar, if desired.
